# Getting Support

Looking for help with Sketchy Cheat Menu Plus? Here's where to go.

## Bug reports

Open an [issue](../../issues/new/choose) and pick the **Bug report**
template. Include your mod version, mod stack, and any relevant
`error.log` snippets.

## Mod-vs-mod conflicts

If the issue only happens when another mod is loaded (an overhaul, a
total conversion, etc.), use the **Compatibility issue** template
instead of the bug template.

The README's [Compatibility section](README.md#compatibility) covers
the load-order mechanic that fixes most overhaul conflicts —
**CleanSlate and Proper4KUI are already handled**.

## Feature requests

Open an [issue](../../issues/new/choose) and pick the **Feature
request** template. Describe what you'd like and what the use-case is.

## "How do I..." questions

Please use [Discussions](../../discussions) rather than opening an
issue. Discussions is the right space for installation help, mod-stack
recommendations, gameplay questions, and general modding chat.

## CK2 modding community

For broader CK2 modding questions not specific to Sketchy Cheat Menu Plus,
the [Paradox CK2 forums](https://forum.paradoxplaza.com/forum/forums/crusader-kings-ii.749/)
remain the long-standing community space.
