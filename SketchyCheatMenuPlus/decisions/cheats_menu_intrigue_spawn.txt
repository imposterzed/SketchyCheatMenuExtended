############################################################
##                Sketchy Cheat Menu Plus                 ##
##             Cheats Menu - Spawn Decisions              ##
############################################################
##                                                        ##
## Sketchy's spawn submenu. cspawn_enable/disable is the  ##
## master toggle; each `## ===== Section ===== ##`        ##
## divider below groups one section (Spawn Children,      ##
## Spawn Siblings, Spawn Courtiers), each with its own    ##
## show/hide_spawn_<X> visibility toggle.                 ##
##                                                        ##
## Every spawned character gets flag = spawned_<X> in     ##
## create_character so finalize_scmp_spawned_character_   ##
## effect (in common/scripted_effects/scmp_spawn_effects. ##
## txt) can find them right after creation.               ##
##                                                        ##
## Children & siblings come in age variants               ##
## (0/5/8/10/12/14/16) for each sex. Courtier templates   ##
## cover the council roles plus a few flavor archetypes.  ##
##                                                        ##
############################################################
