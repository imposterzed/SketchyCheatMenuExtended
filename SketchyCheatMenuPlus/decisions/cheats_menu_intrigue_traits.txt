############################################################
##                Sketchy Cheat Menu Plus                 ##
##              Cheats Menu - Trait Decisions             ##
############################################################
##                                                        ##
## The trait submenu. traits_enable/disable is the master ##
## toggle; each `## ===== Section ===== ##` divider below ##
## groups one trait family (Cheat Traits, Virtues, Sins,  ##
## Personality, Education, Health & Congenital,           ##
## Leadership, Lifestyles, Misc, Vassal, Dynasty &        ##
## Spouse). Every section has its own show_<X>/hide_<X>   ##
## visibility toggle and a pair of add_/remove_<trait>    ##
## decisions per trait.                                   ##
##                                                        ##
############################################################
